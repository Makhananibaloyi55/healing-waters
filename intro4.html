chapter 4: Letting Go Of The Past

Freeing youself from yesterday

The past has a strong grip. Regert, shame, guilt- they can follow you like shadow that never rest. you reply moments, wonder what you could've done differently, and sometimes wish you could go back and change it all.

But healing asks you to stop carring yesterday on you back. you were never meant to live stuck in memories that bring you pain, God invities you into a future that isn't defined by your worst days.

"forget the former things; do not dwell on the past, see, I am doind a new thing!" - Isaiah 43:18-19

When God looks at you , He doesn't see the mistakes, He sees your heart, your growth,and the beauty rising from the ashes. The past may have shaped you, but it does not own you.

This chapter is a reminder that you can leave the prison of yesterday and step into the promised of today.
